This project presents a Train Gate Controller. It harnesses the capabilities of modern technology to enhance the safety and efficiency of railway level crossings. Fire Alarm Project

Fire alarm system is a simple high temperature detector circuit which starts the alarm if the temperature is too high than usual. The system can be used in any room to prevent any kind of unexpected accidental fire. This system will alert the people of that particular area whenever it detects a high temperature difference. This circuit is also self operated and it will not alarm if it detects some high temperature.

Our online ticket booking system for bus, train, and plane tickets is a database system designed to provide a one-stop platform for booking tickets for various modes of transportation.
